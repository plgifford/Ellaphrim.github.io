Step Two: Formulas

Formula for L pieces: L = 4

Formula for T pieces: T = 2l + 2w - 4

Formula for Segments: S = (l + 1) x w + l x (w + 1)

Formula for X pieces: X = (l - 1) x (w - 1)

Step Three: Inventory

A grid with the dimensions of 12 and 32 would have 812 segments, four L joints, 84 T joints, and 341 X joints.
