Perigrine Falcons

Non Magical

Peregrine Falcons are one of the greatest predators of the natrual world. They can reach speeds of up to 320 km/h as they drop towards their prey.

Fast Facts!

  • Perigrines are considered by some the rulers of the bird world.
  • The Perigrine Falcon is found on all continents exepct Antarctica
  • Female perigrines are larger than males
  • Perigrines are used as hunting birds, and are highly prized by falconers
  • In French, perigrines are "Faucon pèlerin". Spanish? "Halcón peregrino"
  • The oldest recorded perigrine was about 19 years and 9 month old when it was identified by its band in 2012, after being tagged in 1992!
